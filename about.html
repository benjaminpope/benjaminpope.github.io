About Me

I grew up in Sydney, New South Wales, and studied for my Honours and Masters at the University of Sydney. I studied abroad at the University of California, Berkeley, and recently completed my DPhil in Astrophysics at Balliol College, Oxford. I'm now a NASA Sagan Fellow at NYU.

Outside of physics, I'm interested in Vikings, language, and economic history. At the moment, I'm especially taken by Robert Macfarlane's The Old Ways and exploring the walking routes of England. I'm vegetarian, keen on Python, Bayes, cycling, coffee, and Earl Grey.

I represented Balliol College in the 2016-17 series of University Challenge on BBC2, with the wonderful Joey Goldman, Freddy Potts, and Jacob Lloyd.
